Introduction

Focus Area and Target Users

Our TLE focus area is all about FCS (Family Consumer Science). The target audience of this prototype is families and communities who want a simple and practical way to learn about making ice cream.

Families & Communities

Why We Chose This Prototype

We chose this kind of prototype because many people cannot afford an ice cream maker machine. Some ice cream makers also use electricity, which may not always be available or practical in some places. We wanted to design a prototype that can work manually even without electricity.

Affordable and Manual Design

Purpose and Importance

The purpose of this prototype is to demonstrate the basic process of making ice cream through a simple freezing method using ice and salt while mixing the ingredients. It is useful as a learning tool in school activities and experiments, and it can also bring people together for a fun and enjoyable bonding experience.

Learning + Fun + Bonding
