User Friendly Instructions

How to use our prototype?

  • Step 1 - open the lid, or cover.
  • Step 2 - remove the metal container.
  • Step 3 - put some ice, and salt in the base.
  • Step 4 - open the metal container.
  • Step 5 - put some milk, or any drinks that you want.
  • Step 6 - close metal container.
  • Step 7 - put the metal container back.
  • Step 8 - put the lid, or cover.
  • Step 9 - roll it for 2-5mins.
  • Step 10 - remove the lid, or cover.
  • Step 11 - take the metal container.
  • Step 12 - open the metal container.
  • Step 13 - put the ice cream in the cup, and enjoy your ice cream.
